Established in 2017, DEHAH Industries Pvt. Ltd

embarked on a trajectory of industry excellence, initially focusing on servicing paper mills beyond India's borders. However, their ambit swiftly expanded to encompass the domestic landscape as well. The company's multifaceted scope is anchored in two pivotal domains.

Firstly, DEHAH offers comprehensive support to nascent paper mill investors, starting from the project's very inception. Their involvement spans the entire project lifecycle, meticulously overseeing each phase. This encompasses the fine-tuned configuration of paper machines through precision engineering, meticulous adherence to project timelines, and the strategic orchestration of dismantling and erection sequences, all under vigilant supervision.

Secondly, DEHAH demonstrates prowess in consultancy services tailored for the paper industry. Their adept guidance is aimed at elevating product imagess and optimizing machinery efficiency, solidifying their commitment to the holistic advancement of the industry.

Our Mission

At the heart of our mission is the drive to pioneer innovative production processes, propelled by ceaseless research and development. We are committed to harnessing eco-friendly raw materials and cutting-edge technology that not only elevate industry standards but also align with the preservation of our planet for the well-being of future generations.

Our Vision

Envisioning a global footprint, we aspire to stand at the forefront of catering to the dynamic demands of the Paper and Paper products market across the world. With an unwavering dedication to excellence, we strive to carve a leadership position in the consultancy domain. Our relentless pursuit of progress extends to the realm of machinery installations and paper production processes, with an emphasis on sustained enhancements.
